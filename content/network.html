Network Diagrams

Many students are not able to demonstrate a mastery of using network diagrams to determine relevant answers to project management questions. This course:

  • clarifies course terminology including earliest start, earliest finish, latest start, and latest finish (ES, EF, LS, LF).
  • breaks down the process steps associated with the forward and backward pass procedures.
  • emphasizes key learning points for the learners.
