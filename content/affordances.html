Affordances

Many educators have had to make changes from in person to online curriculum while adjusting to the Covid-19 pandemic. These educators need a guide to help with this transition. It is important that the needs of the students are still met with the new online instruction.

From the client, iDesign:
“Let's help educators adapt traditional face-to-face practices in a HyFlex/blended format by guiding them through the development of course goals and learning objectives, assignments, assessments, and student support mechanisms with technology integration in mind.”
Outcome

This course helps instructors choose programs that are within their comfort level, considering they may not have experience with online instruction.
